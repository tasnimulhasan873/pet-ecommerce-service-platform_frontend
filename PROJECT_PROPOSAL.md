## 1. PROJECT TITLE

**Puchito: Comprehensive Digital Platform for Veterinary Services and Pet Care Products**

A modern, user-friendly web-based solution that bridges the gap between pet owners, veterinary professionals, and pet care businesses through an integrated online platform.

---

## 2. EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

Puchito is an all-in-one digital platform designed to revolutionize how pet owners access veterinary care and pet products. The system connects three key stakeholders:

- **Pet Owners (Customers)** seeking convenient access to veterinary consultations and quality pet products
- **Veterinary Professionals (Doctors)** looking to expand their reach and streamline their practice management
- **Platform Administrators** managing the entire ecosystem and business operations

The platform combines e-commerce functionality with professional service booking, creating a seamless experience where pet owners can shop for products, book veterinary appointments, complete secure payments, and access educational content—all from a single, easy-to-use website that works on any device (computer, tablet, or smartphone).

**Project Scope:** Full-stack web application with user management, e-commerce, appointment scheduling, payment processing, and comprehensive administrative controls.

**Target Market:** Pet owners, veterinary clinics, pet care businesses, and animal health professionals.

---

## 3. PROBLEM STATEMENT

### Current Challenges in Pet Care Industry

**For Pet Owners:**

- Difficulty finding and booking appointments with qualified veterinarians, especially outside business hours
- Fragmented shopping experience across multiple websites for pet products
- Uncertainty about veterinarian qualifications and service quality
- Lack of convenient payment options and service tracking
- Limited access to reliable pet care information

**For Veterinary Professionals:**

- Heavy reliance on phone-based appointment scheduling consuming valuable time
- Limited online visibility restricting client acquisition
- Manual payment collection and tracking creating administrative burden
- No centralized platform to showcase credentials and attract new clients
- Missing opportunities for remote consultations

**For Pet Care Businesses:**

- Need for integrated platform to manage products, services, and customers
- Difficulty tracking revenue across different income streams
- Complex inventory and order management
- Limited tools for customer retention and engagement
- Inability to analyze business performance in real-time

**Market Gap:** No existing solution comprehensively addresses these challenges in one unified platform specifically designed for the pet care industry.

## 6. SYSTEM WORKFLOW

### 6.1 Customer Journey: Shopping for Pet Products

**Step 1: Discovery**

- Customer visits website homepage
- Browses featured products or uses search function
- Views product categories (food, toys, accessories, health products)

**Step 2: Product Selection**

- Clicks on product to view detailed information
- Reviews product images, descriptions, pricing
- Checks stock availability
- Clicks "Add to Cart" button

**Step 3: Shopping Cart Review**

- Views all items in cart with quantities and prices
- Updates quantities or removes unwanted items
- Sees running total with itemized costs
- Can continue shopping or proceed to checkout

**Step 4: Checkout Process**

- System checks if profile is complete (name, phone, address required)
- If incomplete, redirects to complete profile first
- Once profile complete, proceeds to checkout
- Reviews shipping address and contact information
- Enters promotional coupon code if available

**Step 5: Payment**

- Reviews final order summary
- Enters credit/debit card information through secure payment form
- Completes payment transaction
- Receives instant confirmation on screen

**Step 6: Confirmation & Tracking**

- Receives email confirmation with order details
- Order appears in "My Orders" dashboard
- Tracks order status: Processing → Shipped → Delivered
- Receives updates at each stage

**Step 7: Delivery**

- Product delivered to specified address
- Can review purchase and reorder if satisfied

### 6.2 Customer Journey: Booking Veterinary Appointment

**Step 1: Finding a Doctor**

- Customer navigates to Services section
- Views list of available veterinarians
- Sees doctor photos, names, specialties, and ratings

**Step 2: Doctor Selection**

- Clicks on preferred doctor for detailed profile
- Reviews qualifications, experience, and specialization
- Checks consultation fee in local currency
- Reads doctor's bio and approach to care

**Step 3: Appointment Scheduling**

- Selects preferred appointment date from available dates
- Chooses time slot that fits their schedule
- Provides appointment details (pet name, concern/symptoms)

**Step 4: Profile Verification**

- System checks if account profile is complete
- If incomplete, redirects to update contact information
- Ensures smooth communication and service delivery

**Step 5: Payment**

- Reviews appointment details and consultation fee
- Completes secure payment
- Payment confirmation received

**Step 6: Confirmation**

- Receives booking confirmation with unique appointment ID
- Gets Google Meet link for online consultation
- Appointment details saved in "My Services" dashboard
- Receives email confirmation with all details

**Step 7: Reminder & Appointment**

- Receives reminder notification before appointment time
- Joins consultation via provided meeting link at scheduled time
- Consults with veterinarian about pet's health
- Receives advice, prescription, or follow-up recommendations

**Step 8: Post-Consultation**

- Appointment marked as completed
- Can rebook with same doctor if needed
- Appointment history saved for reference

### 6.3 Doctor Journey: Joining & Managing Practice

**Step 1: Registration**

- Doctor creates account selecting "Doctor" role
- Provides basic information and email

**Step 2: Profile Setup**

- Enters professional details:
  - Full name and contact information
  - Qualifications and degrees
  - License numbers and certifications
  - Years of experience
  - Specialization areas
  - Consultation fee
  - Professional bio
  - Available days and time slots

**Step 3: Verification Wait**

- Submits complete profile for administrator review
- Profile marked as "Pending Verification"
- Waits for credential verification (typically 1-3 business days)

**Step 4: Approval & Activation**

- Administrator reviews credentials and licenses
- If approved, doctor receives verification badge
- Profile becomes visible to customers searching for veterinarians
- If rejected, receives feedback for corrections

**Step 5: Receiving Bookings**

- Customers can now see and book appointments
- Doctor receives instant notification for each new booking
- Appointment details appear in Doctor Dashboard

**Step 6: Managing Appointments**

- Views all appointments in organized calendar view
- Sees patient details and appointment reasons
- Prepares for consultations
- Accesses Google Meet link for online sessions

**Step 7: Conducting Consultations**

- Joins appointment at scheduled time
- Consults with pet owner about concerns
- Provides diagnosis, advice, or prescriptions
- Marks appointment as completed after session

**Step 8: Earnings Tracking**

- Views consultation fees earned
- Monitors daily, weekly, and monthly income
- Tracks pending payments from upcoming appointments
- Downloads financial reports for tax purposes

**Step 9: Schedule Management**

- Updates availability as needed
- System automatically prevents double-booking
- Blocks time slots when unavailable

### 6.4 Administrator Journey: Platform Management

**Step 1: Daily Overview**

- Logs into admin dashboard
- Reviews key metrics:
  - New user registrations
  - Orders placed in last 24 hours
  - Revenue generated
  - Pending doctor verifications
  - Active appointments

**Step 2: Doctor Verification**

- Checks new doctor registration requests
- Reviews submitted credentials and qualifications
- Verifies license numbers and certifications
- Approves qualified doctors or requests corrections

**Step 3: Product Management**

- Adds new products to catalog
- Uploads product images via image hosting service
- Sets pricing in both USD and local currency
- Organizes products into categories
- Updates stock levels
- Removes out-of-stock or discontinued items

**Step 4: Order Processing**

- Reviews new orders
- Updates order statuses as items are processed
- Marks orders as shipped with tracking information
- Resolves any customer issues or concerns
- Processes refunds when necessary

**Step 5: Content Publishing**

- Creates educational blog posts about pet care
- Uploads featured images for articles
- Writes engaging content (pet health tips, training advice, nutrition guides)
- Publishes content visible to all website visitors

**Step 6: Marketing & Promotions**

- Creates coupon codes for seasonal sales
- Sets discount amounts and expiration dates
- Distributes codes through email or social media
- Tracks coupon redemption rates

**Step 7: Financial Review**

- Monitors total platform revenue
- Reviews payment transactions
- Checks for any failed or disputed payments
- Generates financial reports for stakeholders
- Analyzes revenue trends to inform business decisions

**Step 8: User Support**

- Responds to user inquiries or issues
- Resets passwords for users who forgot credentials
- Addresses payment or order concerns
- Bans accounts engaging in fraudulent activity

   - Mobile users access through mobile-responsive website

   - No automatic supplier integration
   - Manual stock updates by administrator
   - No multi-warehouse support

6. **Analytics**

   - Basic analytics and reporting
   - Advanced business intelligence requires additional tools

7. **Customer Communication**

   - Email-based notifications only
   - No SMS or push notification capability
   - No in-platform chat support

8. **Doctor Features**
   - No patient health record system
   - No prescription management tool
   - No appointment calendar sync with external calendars (Google, Outlook)

### Glossary of Terms

**API (Application Programming Interface)**: Connection point allowing different software systems to communicate

**Cloud Hosting**: Internet-based server infrastructure (vs. physical servers in an office)

**Dashboard**: Summary screen showing key information and metrics

**Encryption**: Scrambling data so only authorized parties can read it

**HTTPS/SSL**: Secure connection protocol (padlock icon in browser)

**PCI DSS**: Payment Card Industry security standards for handling credit cards

**Responsive Design**: Website automatically adjusts to fit any screen size

**Session**: Period of time user is logged into system

**Two-Factor Authentication**: Extra security step beyond password

**Unique ID**: Distinct identifier for each record (prevents duplicates)
